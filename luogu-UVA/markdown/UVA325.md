
```cpp

1. is illegal.
1.0e-55 is legal.
e-12 is illegal.
6.5E is illegal.
1e-12 is legal.
+4.1234567890E-99999 is legal.
7.6e+12.5 is illegal.
99 is illegal.
```
