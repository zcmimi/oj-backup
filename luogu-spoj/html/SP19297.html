题目描述

A far away galaxy, H10 has newly been discovered and incorporated into the Commonwealth which participates in the Galactic Wars. The other galaxies have always bullied it by winning the Galactic Wars every year. Frustrated, H10 has come up with a novel strategy to distract the other galaxies. They are planning to send beautiful nymphs into the lands of the other galaxies. The cosmos is arranged so that there is a unique path between any two galaxies (possibly going through other galaxies). The leaders in H10 will select two different galaxies A and B, and send K nymphs to each of the galaxies that is on the unique path from A to B. Initially there are no nymphos on any galaxy. You are the Chief Executive Officer of this project. The leaders will ask you to send these groups of nymphs by giving various instructions of the form (A,B,K). In the process, they may also ask you how many nymphs are currently there in particular galaxies, so that they are able to take smart decisions in the future. Implement this.
