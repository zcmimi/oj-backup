CFPARTY - Party

题目描述

_n_ people came to a party. Then those, who had no friends among people at the party, left. Then those, who had exactly 1 friend among those who stayed, left as well. Then those, who had exactly 2, 3, ..., _n_ - 1 friends among those who stayed by the moment of their leaving, did the same. What is the maximum amount of people that could stay at the party in the end?

输入输出格式

输入格式


The first input line contains one number _t_ — amount of tests (1 t ). Each of the following _t_ lines contains one integer number _n_ (1 n ).

输出格式


For each test output in a separate line one number — the maximum amount of people that could stay in the end.

输入输出样例

输入样例 #1

1
3

输出样例 #1

1
