PUCMM215 - E 23 Stairs pattern

题目描述

In this problem you will work on a plane, where the natural numbers are arranged in a stair pattern. Starting on coordinate (2,1) thru (2,23) are the first 23 natural numbers in sequence, 1 is in (2,1), 2 is in (2,2) and so on...,the next 23 natural numbers (24 to 46) are arranged from (2,24) thru (24,24), the next 23 from (25,24) thru (25,46), a stair pattern where each step is 24 numbers high, this pattern continues infinitely.
