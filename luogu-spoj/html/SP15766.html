SUMSUM - Enjoy Sum with Operations

题目描述

You are given N numbers of the array (N <= 100000), all less than 10^8 and greater than 0. Now, you are given 2 queries: 1. "1 x i" : Change the i-th number to x. (0 <= x <= 10^8) 2. "2 Op i1 i2": Compute the sum of all two elements taken at a time within index i1 to i2(both inclusive) under the operation Op. Op could be XOR,OR or AND. For example, let N=4, Query=3 and "10 20 30 40" be the Initial array. Query: ``` 2 OR 1 3 1 0 1 2 OR 1 3 ``` Answer: ``` 2 OR 1 3--> (10 OR 20) + (20 OR 30) + (10 OR 30) 1 0 1 --> Now array becomes 0 20 30 40 2 OR 1 3--> (0 OR 20) + (20 OR 30) + (0 OR 30) ```
