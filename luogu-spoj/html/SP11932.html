AMZRCK - Amz Rock

题目描述

To many people in many cultures, music is an important part of their way of life. AmzMohammad is a fan of rock music. and he have n rock tracks (labled from 1 to n) now he wanna select a playlist. in his opinion a good playlist is one that have no two successive tracks. in how many ways?

输入输出格式

输入格式


first line = number of test cases each testcase in an integer n(number of tracks)

输出格式


Output number of good playlists he can make. answer is less than 1000000000. it is the only constraint :)

输入输出样例

输入样例 #1

2
1
2

输出样例 #1

2
3
