SBSTR1 - Substring Check (Bug Funny)

题意翻译

给定两个字符串:长度为10的A，长度为5的B，如果B是A的子串，输出1，否则输出0 注意，只允许使用如下语言：Brainf**k, Whitespace和 Intercal. Translated by @稀神探女

题目描述

Given two binary strings, A (of length 10) and B (of length 5), output 1 if B is a substring of A and 0 otherwise. _**Please note, that the solution may only be submitted in the following languages: Brainf\*\*k, Whitespace and Intercal.**_

输入输出格式

输入格式


24 lines consisting of pairs of binary strings A and B separated by a single space.

输出格式


The logical value of: 'B is a substring of A'.

输入输出样例

暂无测试点

说明

Example ------- First two lines of input: 1010110010 10110 1110111011 10011 First two lines of output: 1 0
