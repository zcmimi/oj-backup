MINES4 - Four Mines

题目描述

A Company that Makes Everything (ACME) has entered the surface mining business. They bought a rectangular field which is split into cells, with each cell having a profit value. A mine is a non-empty rectangular region, and the profit of a mine is equal to the sum of the values of all its cells. ACME wants to extract ore from four different non-overlapping mines.
