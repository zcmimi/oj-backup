ZCR - Zen And His Crush

题目描述

Zen has a Crush and is always fantasizing about her. He is so restless now, that he has decided to meet her and express his immense feelings about her. He fears a lot of being rejected that's why he wants to examine his luck. Zen has **N** biased coins where the probability of getting Head from _**i****th**_ coin is **P**i. His luck of being accepted is the probability of getting **exactly K** heads when N coins are tossed simultaneously. Zen is weak in programming and asking for help. Can you help him to calculate his luck?

输入输出格式

输入格式


First Line of the input file contains, **T**-number of test cases. **(1<=T<=100)** Each test case followed by two integers, **N** and **K. (1<=N<=1000 && 0<=K<=N)** Next line contains **N**-space separated real values, **Pi.**

输出格式


For each test case, you have to print desired output(up to **10** decimal places).

输入输出样例

输入样例 #1

13 20.0 0.5 1.0

输出样例 #1

0.5000000000
