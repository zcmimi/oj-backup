ADAGF - Ada and Greenflies

题目描述

As you might already know, Ada the Ladybug is a farmer. Beside of growing vegetable she also breeds greenflies. There are many kinds of greenflies and it is not trivial to breed them since they behave very strangely. Each kind of greenfly is known under a number. As you have multiple greenflies in a single coop, they produce as much juice as the greatest common divisor of their numbers. Ada owns a row of greenflies (of several kinds) and she can split them by a fence, so that always a continuous segment of greenflies will be in a same coop Ada wants to find out the sum of procution of greenflies over all possible continuous segments (of size at least one), can you help her?

输入输出格式

输入格式


The first line contains an integer **1 , the number of greenflies in row.** The second line contains **N** integers 0

输出格式


Print a single line: the sum of production of all continuous segments.
